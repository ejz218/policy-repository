## 1. Purpose

Lehigh University ("Lehigh", "University", "we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy. This policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, retain, and safeguard ("process") information that identifies, relates to, or describes, directly or indirectly, you as an individual ("Personal Data") that we process for the mission-driven purposes described in this policy.

This policy is guided by the principles of **transparency**, **security**, **data minimization**, and **respect for individual privacy**.

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to all Personal Data processed by Lehigh University, directly or through third-party service providers, in connection with the University's academic, research, and service mission.

In the event that applicable law (such as FERPA) requires additional, different, or more stringent measures than this policy, such measures will govern Lehigh's processing of that data.

## 3. Policy Statement

Lehigh University's processing of Personal Data is done in accordance with this policy and applicable law.

> Lehigh University does not sell data and is not a data broker.

### 3.1 Collection of Personal Data

Lehigh University (directly or through third-party service providers) collects Personal Data for various purposes related to our academic, research, and service mission. The types of Personal Data collected include, but are not limited to:

**Personal Information** — data that identifies you or relates to an identifiable individual:

| Category | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| Identifiers | Name, postal address, telephone number, email address |
| Credentials | Lehigh-issued system credentials, Lehigh Identification Number (LIN), Government Issued ID (where applicable) |
| Financial | Credit and debit card number, financial information |
| Academic | Academic information |
| Health | Health information |
| Other | Profile picture, social media account ID, geolocation |

**Other Information** — data that does not reveal your specific identity or directly relate to an identifiable individual:

- Browser and device information, including browsing history and internet activity
- App usage data
- Information collected through cookies, pixel tags, and other technologies
- Campus location information
- Demographic information that does not reveal your specific identity
- Aggregated and de-identified information

> **Sensitive Data:** Some of the information identified above may be considered sensitive data under certain laws. If required under applicable law, we will collect and process sensitive personal data only with your consent. If you choose not to provide some information, we may not be able to provide you with requested features, services, or information.

**Methods of Collection:**

- **Through Our Services:** Enrollment, taking classes, utilizing campus facilities (including networks, card swipes, communication services), our websites, applications, and social media pages.
- **Offline:** Campus visits, events, phone contact, or other offline interactions.
- **From Other Sources:** Public or proprietary databases, joint marketing partners, and when you connect your social media accounts to our services.
- **Website Data Collection:** We collect your IP address, browser type, and track your sessions on lehigh.edu, though we do not attempt to identify you individually.
- **Through University Academic Research:** We may collect information in the course of academic research conducted by Lehigh University faculty.

### 3.2 Use of Personal Data

Lehigh University (directly or through third-party service providers) uses and processes Personal Data for the following purposes, including but not limited to:

- **Providing Services:** To support account management, access to resources, and related communications.
- **Responding to Inquiries:** To respond to your questions, requests, and other inquiries.
- **Completing Transactions:** To complete transactions and provide related services or communications.
- **Sending Administrative Information:** To send you changes to our offerings, terms, conditions, and policies.
- **Promotional Communications:** To send you emails about our services, offerings, new initiatives, and other news, with your consent or where we have a legitimate interest.
- **Personalization:** To analyze your preferences, personalize interactions, and provide tailored content and offers.
- **Data Analysis:** To analyze and improve our services, enhance user experiences, and optimize our website.
- **Aggregating and Anonymizing Data:** We may use and disclose your information in anonymized or aggregate form without limitation, except where prohibited by law.
- **Business and Operational Purposes:** For audits, fraud and security monitoring, developing new offerings, administrative investigations, and safety checks.
- **Research:** The University requires that researchers adequately protect the privacy of Personal Data collected in human subjects research in accordance with applicable law, including informed consent processes.

### 3.3 Disclosure of Personal Data

We share your Personal Data internally within Lehigh (including with our subsidiaries and affiliates) as needed to fulfill our mission. We may also disclose your information in the following circumstances:

- **With Your Consent:** To fulfill the purpose for which you provided it, with your consent and/or for any other purpose disclosed by us when you provide the information.
- **Third-Party Service Providers:** To facilitate services such as academic services, website hosting, data analysis, payment processing, customer service, and email delivery.
- **Third-Party Collaborators:** To engage in educational, research, and other mission-driven purposes with other institutions and collaborators.
- **Promotional Communications:** Lehigh might use third-party services to send you promotional communications consistent with our academic and research mission.
- **Public Disclosure:** If you choose to post information in public areas, such as message boards or social media pages.
- **Legal Process and Compliance:** When necessary to comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes, including responding to governmental or regulatory requests or in connection with claims, disputes, or litigation.
- **Protection of Rights and Safety:** To detect, prevent, or address fraud, security, or technical issues, and to protect our rights, property, and safety. In instances of emergencies involving life and safety, the University may access individual location data in ways deemed appropriate by campus health or safety experts.
- **Research Data:** All human subjects research is required to include an informed consent process that describes how data will or will not be shared or used.
- **Artificial Intelligence:** Lehigh University may use AI technologies to enhance our services. We require that any data used by AI systems is protected with the same security measures as other personal information.

### 3.4 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to collect information about your preferences and site interactions, to improve our website and services. Third parties engaged by Lehigh, such as Google, may use cookies and other technologies to provide measurement services and target ads.

You can disable cookies through your browser options or opt out of the collection of information; however, doing so may impact your ability to access and use certain features of our services.

### 3.5 Security

Lehigh University runs a robust Information Security Program. We use appropriate security measures according to industry standards — including physical, managerial, organizational, and technical safeguards — to protect your Personal Data from loss, damage, unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration.

However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. While Lehigh strives to use commercially reasonable means to protect Personal Data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

### 3.6 Your Consent, Choices, and Access

- **Consent:** By using Lehigh websites or otherwise providing your Personal Data to us, you consent to the processing of your Personal Data in accordance with this policy, as amended, supplemented, or modified from time to time.
- **Opt-Out:** You may opt out of receiving promotional emails from us.
- **Access and Control:** Subject to legal limits and where required by law, you may have the right to request access, review, correct, update, suppress, restrict, or delete your Personal Data held by us, or withdraw your consent to our processing by contacting us in accordance with this policy.

### 3.7 Retention Period

We strive to retain your information only for as long as needed for the purposes for which it was obtained, unless required or allowed by policy or law to keep it for a longer period. Retention periods are documented in our [Record Management and Retention Policy](https://policy.lehigh.edu/record-management-and-retention-policy).

### 3.8 Third-Party Services

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party websites or services linked to our services. We perform data security and privacy due diligence on our third-party service providers and use commercially reasonable efforts to negotiate appropriate contracts to safeguard the privacy and security of Personal Data.

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads based on your use of our services and other websites. You may opt out of this advertising by visiting the provided links.

If you make payments through our services, your information will be collected by a third-party payment service, and the collection and use of your information will be governed by that provider's privacy policy.

### 3.9 Use of Services by Minors

We do not knowingly directly collect information from individuals under 13. In situations involving such individuals, required information is collected from parents or legal guardians. If we learn we have collected personal data from a child under 13 years old without verification of parental consent, we will delete that information.

### 3.10 Jurisdiction and Cross-Border Transfer

Your Personal Data may be stored and processed in any country where we store or process data or where we engage third-party service providers. By using our services, you understand that your information may be transferred to countries outside of your country of residence. We use reasonable safeguards to protect your information during international transfers and do so in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.

### 3.11 Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update or change this policy at any time, and we will provide notice of any such changes as required by law. The date the privacy policy was last updated or reviewed is identified at the top of the page. You should check our Services periodically to see the current policy and any changes we have made to it.
