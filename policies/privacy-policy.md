## 1. Purpose

Lehigh University has and will continue to maintain the integrity and security of institutional, proprietary, and confidential data entrusted to it.

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to all individuals who access, view, update, or release institutional, proprietary, or confidential data held by Lehigh University. Covered data includes, but is not limited to:

- Student records
- Personnel records
- Human subjects research records
- Financial records (both institutional and personal)
- Health care related records

## 3. Policy Statement

The sensitivity of this data is regulated not only by University policies, but also by restrictions set by various federal and state regulations. Lehigh University takes steps to limit access to this information on a need-to-know basis.

Only necessary access to sensitive data is granted to those individuals who have:

1. Undergone proper training in the handling of such data, and
2. Acknowledged the confidentiality of the data.

Anyone viewing, updating, or releasing information of this type for other than officially authorized University business could be held personally liable and subject to criminal and civil penalties.

## 4. Enforcement

Unauthorized access to, disclosure of, or misuse of sensitive data may result in personal liability and exposure to criminal and civil penalties under applicable federal and state law, as well as University disciplinary action.
